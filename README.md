# Employee Attrition Prediction System

## Business Problem Review

Employee attrition refers to the process in which employees leave an organization due to resignation, retirement, or other reasons. In most companies, high attrition is considered a major business challenge because it increases recruitment costs, onboarding efforts, training expenses, and productivity loss. When experienced employees leave, teams may face delays, reduced efficiency, and disruption in daily operations.

Employee attrition is influenced by several demographic, professional, and workplace-related factors. These may include monthly income, overtime, job satisfaction, work-life balance, distance from home, years at company, department, and job role. Since attrition depends on multiple interconnected factors, it becomes difficult for HR teams to identify at-risk employees through manual observation alone.

The purpose of this project is to build a machine learning-based Employee Attrition Prediction System that can analyze historical employee data and predict whether an employee is likely to leave the organization (`Attrition = Yes/No`). This predictive system can help HR departments take proactive retention measures, improve workforce planning, and reduce employee turnover through data-driven decision-making.
